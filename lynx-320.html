Lynx-320 — Industrial 3D Printer

Our Internal Rapid Production Engine.

We use the Lynx-320 to manufacture high-strength functional parts for our customers. It enables us to deliver prototypes and end-use components in days, not weeks.

Key Capabilities

Cloud Native

Receives encrypted print jobs directly from the Unbound3D cloud. No USBs, no local slicing required.

Real-time Telemetry

Streams over 50 sensor data points per second to the platform for predictive maintenance and layer analysis.

Remote Verification

Integrated camera systems allow remote operators to verify first layers and monitor builds from anywhere.

Designed to run every day.

Lynx-320 is the mini workhorse for industrial printing farms. It combines consumer-like speeds with industrial reliability, ensuring warp-free production of PA-CF and PA-GF parts.
