A Production Platform, Not Just a Printer

The LYNX-350 is a production-grade system engineered for high-performance thermoplastics and reinforced composites. It features an integrated material conditioning workflow, a highly controlled thermal environment, and guaranteed dimensional accuracy for batch-to-batch consistency.

Thermal Control System

  • 350°C All-Metal Extruder
  • 150°C High-Density Silicone Heated Bed
  • 70°C Active Chamber via Forced Convection

Absolute thermal stability is critical for engineering polymers. Our active chamber heating eliminates warping and guarantees superior layer adhesion for materials like PA-CF and PPS, ensuring industrial reliability on every print.

Structural Rigidity & Motion

  • Heavy-Duty CoreXY Architecture
  • Triple Independent Z-Axis
  • 40,000 mm/s² Acceleration
  • 650 mm/s Top Speed

Built on a massive rigid frame, the LYNX-350 delivers blistering speeds without compromising dimensional accuracy. The triple Z-axis ensures a perfectly trammed bed under heavy loads.

Precision Calibration

Non-contact eddy current bed scanning (<60 seconds).
Experience fast, repeatable calibration. No manual tuning, no paper tests. Just pure production readiness.

Material Conditioning

Dual spool system with active heated drying (up to 110°C).
Integrated moisture control for hydroscopic nylon and carbon fiber materials, creating a seamless and reliable workflow.

Engineered for Advanced Materials

Extended thermal headroom enables exploration of high-performance polymers such as PEEK and ULTEM under controlled conditions.
